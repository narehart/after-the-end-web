# main world

The discovery of alien technology transformed human society. But we were irresponsible in weilding it. We drastically altered the world using AI, genetic engineering, teleportation, nanotechnology, and other advancements that quickly led to a strange new world and the collapse of organized society. It is in this strange, nightmarish world that our story begins.
